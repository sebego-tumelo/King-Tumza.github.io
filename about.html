About Us

Kingtumza Transport and Projects is a black owned medium company which provides variable trading aspects, construction, transportation, events for corporate and individual management with all other clients projects.

Our Vision

We aim to be amongst one of the best top 20 leading transport companies in Gauteng and grow our multi-disciplinary team in order to offer a broad spectrum of specialist Shuttle Service to become our Clients' preferred Professional Service Provider of choice through excellence and efficiency in all aspects of the traveling cycle.

Our Quality assurance is to ensure that any provision of shuttle services to customers is in accordance with a system which is fair, equitable, transparent, competitive and cost effective.

Our History

Kingtumza transportation was established in 2019 as a Shuttle Service Provider, however due to the changing built environment and the growing needs of Passenger Transportation; it became necessary to broaden the services that could be provided to all customers traveling within the borders of South Africa. Kingtumza transportation has continued to strive towards becoming a complete multi-disciplinary practice offering our customers the specialist individual attention and solutions required by an ever-changing traveling environment and or / atmosphere.
